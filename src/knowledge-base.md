# Support Knowledge Base

*Last Updated: 2026*

This document contains official support policies and troubleshooting guides for Sharar's.

---

## 1\. Account & Login Issues

### Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?

1. Go to the login page.  
2. Click **Forgot Password**.  
3. Enter your registered email address.  
4. Follow the instructions in the reset email.

If you do not receive the email, check your spam or promotions folder.

---

### Q: I'm not receiving the password reset email.

Possible reasons:

- The email was entered incorrectly.  
- The account was created using a different email.  
- The email is in your spam folder.

If the email does not arrive within 10 minutes, contact support.

---

### Q: How do I change my password?

1. Log in to your account.  
2. Go to **Account Settings**.  
3. Click **Change Password**.  
4. Enter your current password and then your new password.  
5. Click **Save Changes**.

---

### Q: I can't log in to my account. What should I do?

1. Make sure you are using the correct email and password.  
2. Try resetting your password using the **Forgot Password** link.  
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again.  
4. Try a different browser or device.

If the issue persists, contact support with your registered email address.

---

## 2\. Course Access Issues

### Q: I purchased a course but cannot access it.

1. Make sure you are logged in with the same email used during purchase.  
2. Go to **My Courses** and check if the course appears there.  
3. Try refreshing the page or logging out and back in.

If the course still does not appear, contact support and provide your order confirmation email.

---

### Q: How do I reset my course progress?

1. Go to **My Courses**.  
2. Open the course you want to reset.  
3. Click **Course Options** or the settings icon.  
4. Select **Reset Progress**.  
5. Confirm the reset.

Note: This action is irreversible. All progress, completed lessons, and quiz scores will be permanently deleted.

---

### Q: My video is not loading or buffering constantly.

1. Check your internet connection speed (minimum 5 Mbps recommended).  
2. Lower the video quality in the player settings.  
3. Clear your browser cache and reload the page.  
4. Disable browser extensions that might block video content.  
5. Try a different browser (Chrome or Firefox recommended).

If the issue continues, contact support and mention your browser version and operating system.

---

### Q: Can I download course videos for offline viewing?

Currently, course videos are not available for offline download. All content must be streamed online through the platform.

---

## 3\. Billing & Refund Issues

### Q: How do I get a refund?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all courses.

To request a refund:

1. Contact support within 30 days of purchase.  
2. Provide your order confirmation email or transaction ID.  
3. State the reason for your refund request.

Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days to the original payment method.

---

### Q: I was charged twice for the same course. What should I do?

1. Check your bank statement to confirm the duplicate charge.  
2. Contact support immediately with:  
   - Your registered email address.  
   - The transaction IDs for both charges.  
   - A screenshot of your bank statement (optional but helpful).

We will investigate and issue a refund for the duplicate charge within 3-5 business days.

---

### Q: How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Log in to your account.  
2. Go to **Account Settings** → **Billing**.  
3. Click **Cancel Subscription**.  
4. Follow the confirmation steps.

Your access will continue until the end of the current billing period. No further charges will be made after cancellation.

Note: Cancelling a subscription does not automatically trigger a refund. If you believe you are eligible for a refund, please contact support.

---

### Q: How do I update my billing information?

1. Log in to your account.  
2. Go to **Account Settings** → **Billing**.  
3. Click **Update Payment Method**.  
4. Enter your new card details and save.

---

## 4\. Technical Issues

### Q: The platform is showing an error message. What should I do?

1. Note the exact error message or take a screenshot.  
2. Refresh the page and try again.  
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies.  
4. Try logging out and back in.  
5. Try a different browser.

If the error persists, contact support with:

- The error message or screenshot.  
- Your browser name and version.  
- Your operating system.

---

### Q: My quiz answers are not being saved.

1. Make sure you have a stable internet connection before submitting.  
2. Do not use the browser back button during a quiz.  
3. Try completing the quiz in a private/incognito browser window.

If the issue continues, contact support with the course name and quiz title.

---

### Q: The code exercises are not working as expected.

1. Make sure you are using the exact version of the tools specified in the course (Node.js, Python, etc.).  
2. Read the error message carefully — it often points to the issue.  
3. Check the course Q\&A section — other students may have had the same problem.  
4. Re-watch the relevant lesson to ensure the setup steps were followed.

If you are still stuck, post your question in the course Q\&A section with your code and the full error message.

---

## 5\. Certificate Issues

### Q: How do I get my completion certificate?

1. Complete all lessons in the course.  
2. Go to **My Courses** and open the completed course.  
3. Click **Get Certificate**.  
4. Download or share your certificate directly from the platform.

Note: All lessons must be marked as complete before the certificate becomes available.

---

### Q: My certificate shows the wrong name. How do I fix it?

1. Go to **Account Settings**.  
2. Update your full name under **Profile Information**.  
3. Save the changes.  
4. Return to your course and regenerate the certificate.

If the name still appears incorrectly, contact support with your preferred name as it should appear on the certificate.

---

## 6\. General Questions

### Q: Can I access the course on multiple devices?

Yes. You can access your courses on any device — desktop, tablet, or mobile — as long as you are logged in with your account.

Simultaneous streaming on multiple devices at the same time is not permitted.

---

### Q: How long do I have access to a course after purchasing?

All course purchases include **lifetime access**. Once you purchase a course, you can access it at any time with no expiry date.

---

### Q: Does the course content get updated?

Yes. Courses are updated periodically to reflect changes in technology and tools. You will have access to all future updates at no additional cost.

---

### Q: How do I contact support?

You can contact support by submitting a ticket through the helpdesk. Our team typically responds within 24-48 business hours.

For urgent billing issues, please mark your ticket as **High Priority**.
